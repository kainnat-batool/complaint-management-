Complaint Management System



This complaint Management system will provide you a facility to send any complaint of the institute at any time from anywhere
This system will work for any student ,staff member and for the parents of students,any Person having any issue related to university can easily access to University administration by using this system.
Once you send have entered your email it will be checked by all the committe members and they will give their solution of that problem to Admin.Admin will tell you about the current status of the Complaint via mail.
And we hope that this system will help both the administration and students to solve the problem by mutual understanding.
